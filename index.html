My projects

During my education, I have done several projects, both alone and in teams with other students.

With some cases we worked with real companies, to help them with a problem, or get a new perspective on a case.

Copenhagen light festival

We brainstormed an idea and produced a website focused on sustainability, fast loading times, and showcasing what CPH Light Festival has to offer. Our approach emphasized eco-friendly practices, ensuring the site loads quickly to enhance user experience, while walking around the city during the festival, and effectively highlighting the festival's unique attractions and offerings.

Link: CPH light festival

Valby IF

We made Valby IF a new website and producing content for it. Our efforts have focused on clearly and invitingly presenting the organization's activities and values, ensuring that both current members and potential new participants can easily find relevant information.

Link: Valby IF

CA A-kasse

We have developed a new website and created content for CA A-kasse to attract new and young students and encourage them to choose CA. Our approach focused on highlighting CA A-kasse's benefits, services, and unique offerings in a clear and engaging manner, ensuring potential students find the information they need easily.

Link: CA kampagne

LeLop redesign

We redesigned Lelop's website from chaotic and confusing to a simpler and clearer interface. Our focus was on providing the relevant information for both visitors and users of the store's recycling stands, ensuring a streamlined user experience and improved accessibility to key details.

Link: LeLop
